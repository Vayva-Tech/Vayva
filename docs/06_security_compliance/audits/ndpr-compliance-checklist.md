## 1. Purpose

This checklist maps the requirements of the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019, the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023, and NITDA implementation guidelines to Vayva's current implementation status. It serves as the primary compliance tracking tool for internal reviews and as preparation for the mandatory annual NDPR audit by a licensed Data Protection Compliance Organization (DPCO).

## 2. Applicability

Vayva Technologies Limited processes personal data of Nigerian data subjects (merchants and their customers) and is therefore subject to the NDPR and NDPA. As a data controller, Vayva must comply with all applicable provisions.

**Classification of Vayva under NDPR:**
- Data Controller: Yes
- Data Processor: Yes (on behalf of merchants for their customer data)
- Annual data processing volume: [To be assessed — determine if Vayva processes data of more than 10,000 data subjects annually, which would require filing a DPCO audit]
