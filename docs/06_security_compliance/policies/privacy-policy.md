## Vayva Technologies Limited — Privacy Policy

Vayva Technologies Limited ("Vayva," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the Vayva commerce platform, our websites, mobile applications, APIs, and related services (collectively, the "Platform").

This policy applies to all users of the Platform, including merchants who operate stores on Vayva and customers who make purchases or interact with merchant stores.

By using the Platform, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Platform.

## 2. What Data We Collect

### 2.1 Data Provided by Merchants

When you register as a merchant, we collect:

- **Identity Information:** Full name, email address, phone number
- **Business Information:** Business name, business registration details, business address, business category
- **Financial Information:** Bank account details for payment settlement (processed securely via Paystack)
- **Store Configuration:** Product listings, pricing, store settings, branding assets
- **AI Configuration:** Custom instructions, tone preferences, and product knowledge provided for AI assistant setup

### 2.2 Data Provided by Customers

When you interact with a merchant store on Vayva, we collect:

- **Identity Information:** Name, email address, phone number
- **Delivery Information:** Delivery address, delivery instructions
- **Transaction Information:** Order details, purchase history, payment status
- **Communication Data:** Messages exchanged with merchant stores via WhatsApp or the platform chat, including AI-assisted conversations

### 2.3 Data Collected Automatically

When you use the Platform, we automatically collect:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, browser type and version
- **Usage Information:** Pages visited, features used, time spent on the Platform
- **Network Information:** IP address, approximate location (city/region level)
- **Session Data:** Authentication tokens, session identifiers

### 2.4 Data from Third Parties

We may receive data from:

- **Paystack:** Transaction confirmation, payment status, and settlement information
- **WhatsApp (via Evolution API):** Message delivery status, read receipts (where available)

## 3. How We Use Your Data

### 3.1 Purpose of Processing

| Purpose | Data Used | Legal Basis |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Account creation and management | Identity, business information | Performance of contract |
| Order processing and fulfillment | Identity, delivery, transaction data | Performance of contract |
| Payment processing | Financial data (via Paystack) | Performance of contract; legal obligation |
| AI-powered customer support | Communication data, order history | Consent (merchant-enabled); legitimate interest |
| WhatsApp messaging | Phone number, message content | Consent |
| Platform improvement and analytics | Usage data (anonymized) | Legitimate interest |
| Security and fraud prevention | Device, network, session data | Legitimate interest; legal obligation |
| Customer support | Identity, transaction, communication data | Performance of contract |
| Legal and regulatory compliance | As required | Legal obligation |
| Marketing communications | Email, phone number | Consent |

### 3.2 AI Processing

Vayva uses artificial intelligence to power merchant customer support and sales assistance. When AI features are enabled by a merchant:

- Customer messages may be processed by AI models to generate responses
- AI processing uses GPT-4o Mini (provided by OpenRouter) and Llama 3.3 70B (provided by OpenRouter)
- Conversation data is transmitted to OpenRouter's servers for processing
- AI-generated responses are reviewed and can be overridden by merchants
- Conversation logs are retained for 90 days, after which they are automatically deleted
- Anonymized conversation summaries may be retained for service improvement

**Your rights regarding AI processing:**
- You may request that your conversations not be processed by AI (contact the merchant or our DPO)
- You may request deletion of your AI conversation history
- You have the right to know when you are communicating with an AI system

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA), we process your data based on:

1. **Consent:** Where you have given clear, informed consent for specific processing (e.g., WhatsApp messaging, marketing emails)
2. **Contract:** Where processing is necessary to fulfill our contractual obligations to you (e.g., processing orders, managing accounts)
3. **Legal Obligation:** Where processing is required by Nigerian law (e.g., financial record-keeping, tax obligations)
4. **Legitimate Interest:** Where processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests, provided these do not override your rights (e.g., security monitoring, anonymized analytics)

## 5. Third-Party Data Sharing

We share your data with the following third parties, only to the extent necessary for the stated purposes:

### 5.1 Payment Processing — Paystack

- **Data Shared:** Transaction amount, currency, customer email, payment method details
- **Purpose:** Payment processing and settlement
- **Safeguards:** Paystack is PCI DSS Level 1 certified. Payment card data is handled entirely by Paystack and never stored on Vayva systems
- **Privacy Policy:** https://paystack.com/privacy

### 5.2 AI Processing — OpenRouter

- **Data Shared:** Conversation content (customer messages and context), merchant product information
- **Purpose:** AI-generated responses for customer support and sales assistance
- **Safeguards:** Data Processing Agreement in place; data transmitted over encrypted channels; conversations are not used to train third-party models without consent
- **Privacy Policy:** https://openrouter.ai/privacy

### 5.3 WhatsApp Messaging — Evolution API (Self-Hosted)

- **Data Shared:** Phone numbers, message content, media attachments
- **Purpose:** Enabling WhatsApp-based customer communication for merchants
- **Safeguards:** Evolution API is self-hosted on Vayva's infrastructure; messages encrypted in transit; message logs retained for 90 days
- **Note:** WhatsApp's own privacy terms also apply to messages sent via WhatsApp

### 5.4 Hosting and Infrastructure — Vercel

- **Data Shared:** Frontend application traffic, potentially including IP addresses and request metadata
- **Purpose:** Hosting and serving the Vayva frontend applications
- **Safeguards:** Vercel is SOC 2 Type II certified; data encrypted in transit
- **Privacy Policy:** https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

### 5.5 Other Disclosures

We may disclose personal data:

- To law enforcement or regulatory bodies when required by law or valid legal process
- To protect the rights, property, or safety of Vayva, our users, or the public
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with prior notice to affected users)

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

| Data Category | Retention Period |

## 8. Your Rights

Under the NDPR and NDPA, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 8.1 Right of Access
You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. We will provide it in a commonly used electronic format (JSON or CSV) within 30 days.

### 8.2 Right to Rectification
You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data. You can update most information directly through your account dashboard.

### 8.3 Right to Erasure
You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to our legal retention obligations. Account deletion can be initiated from your account settings or by contacting the DPO.

### 8.4 Right to Restrict Processing
You may request that we limit the way we use your data in certain circumstances.

### 8.5 Right to Data Portability
You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format for transfer to another service provider.

### 8.6 Right to Object
You may object to processing based on legitimate interest. We will stop processing unless we demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds.

### 8.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

### 8.8 Right Regarding Automated Decision-Making
You have the right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing (including AI) that significantly affect you, and to request human review of such decisions.

### How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact our Data Protection Officer:
- **Email:** dpo@vayva.africa
- **Platform:** Use the "Privacy & Data" section in your account settings
- **Response Time:** Within 30 days of receiving your verified request

If you are unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with NITDA:
- **Website:** https://nitda.gov.ng
- **Email:** info@nitda.gov.ng

- Regular security assessments and vulnerability scanning
- Incident detection and response procedures
- Employee security awareness training
- Secure software development practices

For full details, refer to our Information Security Policy.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The Vayva Platform is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will promptly delete it.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact our DPO immediately.
