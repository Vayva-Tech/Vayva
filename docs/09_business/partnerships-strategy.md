## 1. Partnership Philosophy

Vayva's partnership strategy is built on a simple principle: **partner with companies that make our merchants more successful.** Every partnership should either (a) bring more merchants to Vayva, (b) make the merchant experience better, or (c) reduce our costs. Ideally, all three.

We do not pursue partnerships for press releases or logos on a website. Every partnership must deliver measurable value to merchants and to Vayva.

## 4. Logistics & Delivery Partnerships

### 4.1 Partnership Model

Vayva does not handle logistics directly. Instead, we integrate with logistics partners so merchants can arrange delivery from within their dashboard and AI agents can provide customers with delivery estimates and tracking.

### 4.2 Priority Partners

#### GIG Logistics
- **Why:** Largest logistics network in Nigeria. Nationwide coverage including tier-2/3 cities
- **Integration:** API for quote generation, booking, and tracking
- **Value Exchange:** Vayva drives booking volume; GIG provides reliable delivery for merchants
- **Timeline:** Q2 integration

#### Kwik Delivery
- **Why:** Same-day delivery in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt. Strong for food/grocery
- **Integration:** Real-time delivery tracking, in-app booking
- **Value Exchange:** Vayva drives order volume; Kwik provides fast delivery for time-sensitive verticals
- **Timeline:** Q2 integration

#### Sendbox
- **Why:** Multi-carrier aggregator. Gives merchants options for best price/speed
- **Integration:** Shipping rate comparison, label generation, tracking
- **Value Exchange:** Vayva provides merchant base; Sendbox provides logistics flexibility
- **Timeline:** Q3 integration

### 4.3 Logistics Integration Vision

The end goal is for Vayva's AI agent to handle the full post-purchase experience:

1. Customer places order via WhatsApp AI agent
2. AI agent confirms order and processes payment (Paystack)
3. AI agent automatically books delivery with the merchant's preferred logistics partner
4. AI agent sends tracking link to customer
5. AI agent follows up after delivery for feedback

**This makes Vayva the complete commerce operating system** -- merchants never need to leave the platform.

## 5. WhatsApp BSP Partnerships

### 5.1 Current: Evolution API

**Relationship Type:** WhatsApp API infrastructure
**Status:** Active
**Cost:** Self-hosted, minimal per-message costs

**Current Setup:**
- Self-hosted Evolution API instance for WhatsApp connectivity
- Handles message sending, receiving, and media
- Supports multi-device and multi-merchant connections

### 5.2 Future: Official WhatsApp BSP

As Vayva scales, transitioning to an official WhatsApp Business Solution Provider (BSP) partnership provides:
- Higher message throughput
- Better deliverability and reliability
- Access to WhatsApp Business features (catalogs, flows, payments)
- Compliance with Meta's commercial policies

**Potential BSP Partners:**
| Partner | Strengths | Consideration |
|---|---|---|
| **360dialog** | Direct Meta partnership, competitive pricing | German-based, limited African support |
| **Twilio** | Reliable, well-documented API | Expensive at scale |
| **MessageBird** | Strong API, good pricing | European focus |
| **Infobip** | African presence, multi-channel | Complex integration |

**Decision Timeline:** Q3-Q4, based on message volume and reliability requirements.

### 5.3 Meta/WhatsApp Direct Partnership

**Long-term Goal:** Establish a direct relationship with Meta/WhatsApp for:
- Early access to WhatsApp Business features
- Featured partner status in WhatsApp Business directory
- Potential integration with WhatsApp Payments (when available in Nigeria)
- Co-marketing as a WhatsApp commerce success story

## 10. Partnership Tiers

### 10.1 Tier Structure

| Tier | Criteria | Benefits for Partner | Vayva Commitment |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Referral** | Refers merchants to Vayva | 10% revenue share (3 months) | Custom referral link, basic reporting |
| **Integration** | Technical integration with Vayva platform | API access, technical support, co-marketing | Dedicated integration support, joint documentation |
| **Strategic** | Deep business relationship, mutual growth | Revenue share, co-marketing, joint product development | Dedicated partner manager, quarterly business reviews |
| **Foundational** | Core infrastructure partner | Preferred status, volume pricing, board-level relationship | Executive sponsor, monthly syncs, strategic alignment |

### 10.2 Partner Onboarding Process

1. **Discovery:** Identify mutual value and alignment
2. **Proposal:** Draft partnership terms and success metrics
3. **Legal:** Execute partnership agreement (standard template, custom for Strategic/Foundational)
4. **Integration:** Technical integration (if applicable) with dedicated support
5. **Launch:** Co-marketing announcement, internal training
6. **Review:** Monthly check-ins (Strategic/Foundational), quarterly check-ins (Integration/Referral)
