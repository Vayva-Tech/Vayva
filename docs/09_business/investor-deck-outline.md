## Slide 2: The Problem

### Nigerian merchants are losing billions in sales because they cannot respond fast enough.

**The reality of African commerce:**
- 90% of Nigerian online commerce happens through WhatsApp and social media
- Merchants receive hundreds of inquiries daily but can only respond to a fraction
- Average response time: 2-6 hours. 60% of buyers have moved on within 30 minutes.
- Result: Nigerian SMBs lose an estimated 20-40% of potential revenue to response delays

**The human bottleneck:**
- A fashion merchant in Lagos gets 200 WhatsApp messages daily
- She can personally respond to maybe 50 during business hours
- 150 potential customers get no response or a delayed response
- Those are sales walking out the door every single day

**Supporting data points:**
- 41.5 million MSMEs in Nigeria (SMEDAN)
- WhatsApp is used by 93% of Nigerian internet users
- $12B+ Nigerian e-commerce market growing at 15-20% CAGR
- Informal social commerce is estimated at 3-5x the formal e-commerce figure

## Slide 3: The Solution

**What merchants experience:**

**Setup time:** Under 10 minutes from signup to live AI agent

## Presentation Guidelines

### For the Founder:
1. **Lead with the story.** Open with a real merchant's experience -- the frustration, the lost sales, the moment Vayva changed everything.
2. **Demo the product.** Live demo beats slides every time. Show a real AI agent conversation.
3. **Know the numbers cold.** Unit economics, market size, growth projections -- be ready to discuss without looking at slides.
4. **Be honest about stage.** Early-stage investors want to see founder quality and market insight, not perfection.
5. **End with vision.** Paint the picture of what African commerce looks like with Vayva at scale.

### Deck Versions:
- **Full deck (14+ slides):** For formal pitch meetings with VCs
- **Short deck (8 slides):** For angel investors and first conversations
- **One-pager:** For cold outreach and email introductions
- **Data room:** Detailed financials, legal docs, technical architecture -- for due diligence
