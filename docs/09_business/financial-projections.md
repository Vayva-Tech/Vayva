## 1. Executive Summary

This document presents 12-month and 36-month financial projections across three scenarios: conservative, moderate, and aggressive. Under the moderate scenario, Vayva reaches **₦40.2M MRR by Month 12** (1,000 total merchants), achieves **operating breakeven by Month 10**, and generates **₦482M ARR** exiting Year 1. By Month 36, the platform projects **₦2.1B MRR** with **10,000+ active merchants** and operating margins exceeding 60%.

## 11. Key Assumptions & Risks

### 11.1 Revenue Assumptions

| Assumption | Base Case | Downside Risk | Upside Potential |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monthly merchant growth | 18--22% | Could slow to 8--12% if PMF is weaker | Could accelerate to 30%+ with viral loop |
| Active rate | 75% | Could drop to 60% with poor onboarding | Could reach 85% with strong activation |
| Monthly churn | 6--8% | Could spike to 12%+ in early months | Could stabilize at 3--4% with sticky product |
| Blended ARPU growth | 5% quarterly | Could stagnate if merchants stay on STARTER | Could grow 10%+ with strong upgrade path |
| Withdrawal fee capture | 60--70% of GMV | Could be lower if merchants prefer direct pay | Could be higher with wallet incentives |

### 11.2 Expense Assumptions

| Assumption | Base Case | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| AI model pricing | Stable or declining | Could increase if OpenRouter changes terms |
| Naira/USD rate | ₦1,550 | Devaluation increases all USD costs |
| Hiring costs | Lagos market rates | Talent competition could inflate salaries |
| Infrastructure scaling | Linear with merchants | Unexpected scaling challenges could spike costs |

### 11.3 Risk Mitigation Matrix

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Slow merchant acquisition | Medium | High | Multi-channel marketing, referral program, free trial |
| High early churn | Medium | High | Onboarding optimization, customer success team, feature gating |
| AI cost escalation | Low | Medium | Model optimization, caching, fine-tuning, on-premise inference |
| Naira devaluation (>30%) | Medium | Medium | USD reserves, annual prepayments, local alternatives |
| Competitor entry | Medium | Medium | Speed to market, feature moat, community building |
| Regulatory changes | Low | High | Legal counsel, compliance-first approach, industry association membership |
