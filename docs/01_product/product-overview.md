# Vayva Product Overview

> Last updated: 2026-03-23

## What is Vayva?

Vayva is an AI-powered commerce SaaS platform purpose-built for the Nigerian and African market. It gives merchants a complete digital infrastructure to sell online and offline -- from branded storefronts and AI-driven customer engagement to payment processing, inventory management, and multi-channel sales -- all accessible through a single dashboard at `merchant.vayva.ng`.

Vayva is not a marketplace. Each merchant gets their own store, their own AI sales agent, and their own customer relationships. The platform handles the technology so merchants can focus on their business.

---

## Who is Vayva For?

**Primary audience:** Nigerian small and medium businesses that sell products or services and want to:

- Accept orders online without building a website from scratch
- Automate customer conversations on WhatsApp (the dominant messaging channel in Nigeria)
- Get AI-powered insights to grow revenue
- Process payments in Naira via Paystack
- Manage their entire business from one dashboard

**Secondary audiences:**

- Businesses across other African markets (Ghana, Kenya, South Africa) -- the platform supports multi-currency pricing
- Specific industry verticals including restaurants, fashion, events, real estate, healthcare, education, nonprofits, and 15+ more

## AI Agent Capabilities

Vayva's AI Agent is the core differentiator. It serves as an always-on sales assistant that engages customers via WhatsApp and webchat.

### What the AI Agent Does

- **Answers product questions** -- uses the merchant's catalog to provide accurate product information, pricing, availability, and descriptions
- **Handles order placement** -- guides customers through selecting products and placing orders
- **Provides recommendations** -- suggests relevant products based on customer queries and purchase history
- **Manages FAQs** -- answers common questions using the merchant's knowledge base
- **Processes order status inquiries** -- looks up order status and provides tracking information
- **Handles payment guidance** -- directs customers to payment methods
- **Escalates to humans** -- routes complex conversations to merchant staff when needed

### AI Agent Personalization

Merchants can fully customize their AI agent through `MerchantAiProfile`:

| Setting | Options |
|---------|---------|
| Agent Name | Custom name (e.g., "Ade" or "VayvBot") |
| Tone Preset | Friendly, Professional, Luxury, Playful, Minimal |
| Greeting Template | Custom welcome message |
| Sign-off Template | Custom conversation closing |
| Persuasion Level | 0 (informational) to 3 (active selling) |
| Brevity Mode | Short or Medium response length |
| Escalation Rules | When to hand off to a human |
| Prohibited Claims | Things the AI should never say |
| Language | Preferred response language |

### AI Autopilot (PRO and PRO_PLUS)

The Autopilot engine runs daily analysis of merchant data and generates AI-powered recommendations:

- **Inventory optimization** -- identifies dead stock, slow movers, overstock, and low-stock items
- **Marketing suggestions** -- flags weak product descriptions, poor SEO titles, and flash sale candidates
- **Customer engagement** -- identifies dormant customers, VIP customers, abandoned carts
- **Operations** -- monitors prep times (restaurants), no-shows (events), empty slots (bookings)
- **Industry-specific rules** -- tailored recommendations based on the merchant's industry vertical

All autopilot actions are proposed with status `PROPOSED` for merchant review before execution.

## Onboarding Flow

New merchants go through a guided setup wizard:

1. **Welcome** -- introduction to the platform
2. **Business Details** -- business name, description, contact information
3. **Industry Selection** -- choose industry vertical (determines available features)
4. **Brand Identity** -- logo, colors, brand assets
5. **First Product** -- add the first product or service
6. **Payment Setup** -- configure Paystack for receiving payments
7. **Store Policies** -- set return, shipping, and privacy policies
8. **Social Media** -- connect social accounts
9. **KYC Verification** -- identity verification for payouts
10. **Review** -- review all settings
11. **Publish** -- go live

Specialized setup steps are available for B2B businesses, events, and nonprofits. An interactive tutorial guides first-time users after onboarding is complete.

## Security and Compliance

- **Multi-tenant isolation** -- all data is scoped by `storeId`; cross-tenant access is architecturally prevented
- **Authentication** -- JWT-based sessions with 30-minute idle timeout, bcrypt password hashing
- **Role-based access control** -- OWNER, ADMIN, STAFF roles with granular permissions
- **KYC verification** -- identity verification required for payouts
- **PII protection** -- personal data (emails, phones, card numbers) is stripped before sending to AI APIs
- **Audit logging** -- comprehensive audit trail for all sensitive operations
- **NDPR compliance** -- designed for Nigerian Data Protection Regulation compliance
- **API key security** -- hashed storage, IP allowlists, expiration support
- **Rate limiting** -- tier-based rate limiting (Redis-backed in production)
- **Abuse detection** -- automated signup abuse signals and rule-based fraud prevention
