## Product Philosophy

### Local First
Built specifically for Nigerian businesses with local payment methods, delivery options, and support.

### AI-Powered
Leveraging AI to automate commerce tasks, from order capture to customer service.

### All-in-One
Replacing fragmented tools with a unified platform that handles everything from storefront to delivery.

### Accessible
Affordable pricing tiers that grow with the business, from free tier to enterprise.
