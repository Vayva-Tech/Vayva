## What is Vayva?

**Vayva** is an all-in-one commerce platform designed specifically for Nigerian businesses. It enables merchants to sell products, manage orders, process payments, and handle deliveries through a unified dashboard.

## Mission

Empower Nigerian businesses with world-class commerce tools that are locally relevant, affordable, and easy to use.

## Target Market

### Primary Users

| Segment | Description | Use Case |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Small Businesses** | Local retailers, boutiques | Online storefront, WhatsApp orders |
| **Food Vendors** | Restaurants, caterers | Menu management, delivery tracking |
| **Service Providers** | Salons, consultants | Booking management, appointment scheduling |
| **Event Organizers** | Ticketing, venues | Event sales, QR check-in |
| **B2B Companies** | Wholesalers, distributors | Bulk pricing, quote management |
| **Nonprofits** | Charities, NGOs | Donation campaigns, donor management |

### Geographic Focus

- **Primary:** Nigeria
- **Currency:** Nigerian Naira (₦)
- **Payments:** Paystack (local integration)
- **Delivery:** Kwik (local logistics)

Unique AI-powered WhatsApp commerce:

- **AI Order Capture** - Convert conversations to structured orders
- **Catalog Sync** - Products automatically available on WhatsApp
- **Automated Responses** - 24/7 customer service
- **Order Updates** - Automatic status notifications

### 3. Local Optimizations

- **Paystack Integration** - Seamless Nigerian payments
- **Kwik Delivery** - Local logistics integration
- **Naira Pricing** - Local currency throughout
