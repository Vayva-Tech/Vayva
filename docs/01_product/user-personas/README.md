# User Personas

**Owner:** Nyamsi Fredrick, Founder  
**Last Updated:** March 2026

## Overview

Understanding our users is critical to building the right features. This document defines the key personas who interact with the Vayva platform.

## Merchant Personas

### 1. Ada - The Fashion Entrepreneur

**Demographics:**
- Age: 28
- Location: Lagos
- Business: Online fashion boutique
- Revenue: ₦500k-1M/month

**Goals:**
- Grow online sales
- Manage orders efficiently
- Build customer relationships

**Pain Points:**
- Managing orders across WhatsApp and Instagram
- Keeping track of inventory
- No time for manual processes

**Tech Savvy:** Medium
**Primary Channel:** WhatsApp
**Plan:** Growth

**Quote:** *"I need something that works with how my customers already shop - on WhatsApp."*

---

### 2. Chidi - The Restaurant Owner

**Demographics:**
- Age: 35
- Location: Abuja
- Business: Restaurant & delivery
- Revenue: ₦2-5M/month

**Goals:**
- Streamline delivery orders
- Reduce order errors
- Expand customer base

**Pain Points:**
- Phone orders are chaotic
- Delivery coordination is hard
- No visibility into business metrics

**Tech Savvy:** Low
**Primary Channel:** Phone/WhatsApp
**Plan:** Starter

**Quote:** *"I just want to focus on cooking, not managing technology."*

---

### 3. Fatima - The B2B Distributor

**Demographics:**
- Age: 42
- Location: Kano
- Business: Wholesale electronics
- Revenue: ₦10M+/month

**Goals:**
- Manage bulk orders
- Track credit accounts
- Scale operations

**Pain Points:**
- Complex pricing for different customers
- Managing credit and payments
- Coordinating large deliveries

**Tech Savvy:** High
**Primary Channel:** Web
**Plan:** Enterprise

**Quote:** *"I need enterprise-grade tools that understand Nigerian B2B business."*

---

### 4. Emeka - The Side Hustler

**Demographics:**
- Age: 25
- Location: Port Harcourt
- Business: Sneaker reselling
- Revenue: ₦100-300k/month

**Goals:**
- Test business ideas
- Make extra income
- Learn entrepreneurship

**Pain Points:**
- Limited budget
- No technical skills
- Need to validate demand

**Tech Savvy:** Medium
**Primary Channel:** Instagram
**Plan:** Free

**Quote:** *"I want to start small and grow. I can't afford expensive tools yet."*

## Customer Personas

### 1. Ngozi - The Busy Professional

**Demographics:**
- Age: 32
- Location: Lagos
- Income: ₦500k+/month

**Goals:**
- Shop conveniently
- Get quality products
- Save time

**Behaviors:**
- Shops on mobile
- Prefers WhatsApp communication
- Values fast delivery

**Pain Points:**
- No time to visit physical stores
- Worried about online scams
- Wants easy returns

**Quote:** *"I want to order quickly on WhatsApp and get it delivered the same day."*

---

### 2. Ibrahim - The Price-Conscious Shopper

**Demographics:**
- Age: 26
- Location: Ibadan
- Income: ₦150-250k/month

**Goals:**
- Find best deals
- Compare prices
- Buy quality at low prices

**Behaviors:**
- Researches before buying
- Uses discount codes
- Checks multiple stores

**Pain Points:**
- Limited budget
- Worried about quality
- Shipping costs too high

**Quote:** *"I always look for discounts and compare prices before buying."*

---

### 3. Yemi - The Loyal Customer

**Demographics:**
- Age: 38
- Location: Lagos
- Income: ₦800k+/month

**Goals:**
- Buy from trusted sellers
- Get personalized service
- Build relationships

**Behaviors:**
- Repeat purchases
- Recommends to friends
- Engages on social media

**Pain Points:**
- Inconsistent service
- Hard to track orders
- Impersonal experiences

**Quote:** *"I stick with sellers who know me and my preferences."*

## Operator Personas

### 1. Tolu - The Support Agent

**Demographics:**
- Age: 24
- Role: Customer Support
- Experience: 1 year

**Goals:**
- Resolve tickets quickly
- Help merchants succeed
- Learn the platform

**Pain Points:**
- Need quick access to merchant data
- Complex issues take time
- Repetitive questions

**Quote:** *"I need to see everything about a merchant to help them fast."*

---

### 2. Amaka - The Platform Admin

**Demographics:**
- Age: 31
- Role: Operations Manager
- Experience: 5 years

**Goals:**
- Monitor platform health
- Manage incidents
- Optimize operations

**Pain Points:**
- Too many dashboards
- Need automated alerts
- Complex troubleshooting

**Quote:** *"I need one place to see everything and act quickly when issues arise."*

## Using Personas

### For Product
- Prioritize features based on persona needs
- Design for primary personas first
- Validate with real users matching personas

### For Marketing
- Create targeted campaigns per persona
- Use appropriate channels
- Address specific pain points

### For Support
- Train agents on persona characteristics
- Customize communication style
- Anticipate common issues
