## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How does the free trial work?**
A: You get 7 days of full access to Starter/Pro features. No credit card required. If you don't subscribe after the trial, your account continues on the Free plan.

**Q: Can I switch plans?**
A: Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime from your dashboard.

**Q: Are there transaction fees?**
A: Vayva doesn't charge per-transaction fees. Standard Paystack processing fees apply (1.5% + ₦100 for local cards). Withdrawals from your Vayva wallet incur a 3% fee.

**Q: What happens if I exceed limits?**
A: You'll be notified and can upgrade or wait until next billing cycle.

**Q: Do I need design or coding skills?**
A: Not at all. Vayva includes professional templates with visual drag-and-drop editing. Setup takes under 5 minutes.

**Q: Is there a setup fee?**
A: No setup fees on any plan.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: 14-day money-back guarantee on paid plans.

**Q: What payment methods can my customers use?**
A: Visa, Mastercard, Verve cards, bank transfers, USSD codes, and mobile money. International customers can pay with dollar cards.
