# Marketing Strategist - Role Documentation

## 📈 **POSITION OVERVIEW**
**Reports to:** Head of Marketing/CMO  
**Team Collaboration:** Works with Product, Sales, Design, and Executive teams  
**Project:** Vayva Multi-Industry Business Platform Marketing Strategy  

## 🎯 **PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES**

### **Market Analysis & Positioning**
- Conduct comprehensive competitive analysis across 22 industry verticals
- Develop positioning strategy highlighting Vayva's unique value proposition
- Create buyer personas for each industry segment (Retail owners, Legal firms, Creative agencies, etc.)
- Analyze market trends and identify growth opportunities

### **Go-to-Market Strategy**
- Design integrated marketing campaigns for platform launch
- Develop pricing strategy and packaging for different industry segments
- Create demand generation programs targeting specific verticals
- Build partner ecosystem and referral programs

### **Content & Campaign Management**
- Lead content strategy for marketing website and promotional materials
- Manage digital marketing campaigns across multiple channels
- Coordinate with design team for brand-consistent marketing assets
- Oversee marketing automation and lead nurturing programs

## 🎯 **TARGET MARKETS & SEGMENTS**

### **Primary Industries (High Priority)**
1. **Professional Services** (Law firms, consulting, accounting)
2. **Creative Industries** (Design studios, photography, agencies)
3. **Retail & E-commerce** (Brick-and-mortar, online stores)
4. **Healthcare** (Clinics, practices, medical facilities)
5. **Education** (Schools, training centers, online courses)

### **Secondary Markets (Expansion)**
6. **Real Estate** (Agencies, property management)
7. **Hospitality** (Restaurants, hotels, venues)
8. **Nonprofit Organizations** (Charities, foundations)
9. **Automotive** (Dealerships, repair shops)
10. **Technology** (SaaS companies, startups)

### **Market Segmentation Strategy**
- **Enterprise ($500+/month):** Large organizations with complex needs
- **Business ($199/month):** Medium businesses seeking professional tools
- **Startup ($99/month):** Small businesses and entrepreneurs
- **Freelancer ($49/month):** Individual professionals and solopreneurs

## 📊 **MARKETING CHANNELS & STRATEGIES**

### **Digital Marketing**
- **SEO/SEM:** Industry-specific keyword targeting, Google Ads campaigns
- **Content Marketing:** Blog posts, case studies, whitepapers for each vertical
- **Social Media:** LinkedIn (B2B), Instagram/TikTok (Creative), Facebook (Local Business)
- **Email Marketing:** Segmented campaigns, newsletters, product updates

### **Performance Marketing**
- **PPC Advertising:** Google Ads, Microsoft Advertising, industry-specific platforms
- **Retargeting:** Website visitors, abandoned cart recovery
- **Affiliate Marketing:** Partner programs for industry influencers
- **Referral Programs:** Customer-to-customer acquisition incentives

### **Demand Generation**
- **Webinars:** Industry-specific educational content
- **Free Trials:** 14-day premium access for qualified leads
- **Case Studies:** Success stories from early adopters
- **Partnerships:** Integration with complementary business tools

## 📋 **MARKETING CAMPAIGNS**

### **Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Months 1-2)**
**Goals:** Build awareness, generate interest, collect leads
- **Content Creation:** 20+ industry-specific landing pages
- **SEO Foundation:** Keyword research and technical SEO setup
- **Lead Magnets:** Industry guides, ROI calculators, template libraries
- **Community Building:** Social media presence, LinkedIn groups

### **Phase 2: Launch Campaign (Months 3-4)**
**Goals:** Drive conversions, establish thought leadership
- **Launch Event:** Virtual product launch with demos
- **Press Release:** Industry publications and tech media
- **Influencer Partnerships:** Industry experts and reviewers
- **Paid Advertising:** Multi-channel campaign activation

### **Phase 3: Growth & Retention (Months 5-6)**
**Goals:** Scale acquisition, improve retention, expand usage
- **Customer Success Stories:** Video testimonials and case studies
- **Advanced Segmentation:** Behavior-based marketing automation
- **Product-Led Growth:** In-app upgrades and expansion opportunities
- **Community Engagement:** User groups, feedback programs
