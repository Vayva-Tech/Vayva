# Merchant FAQ

> Comprehensive answers to frequently asked questions about the Vayva platform.

---

## Getting Started

### Q1: What is Vayva?

Vayva is an AI-powered commerce platform built for African businesses. It gives you a professional online storefront, an AI sales agent on WhatsApp, product and order management, and payment processing via Paystack — all in one place. Your storefront is available at `yourstore.vayva.ng`.

### Q2: How do I create my Vayva account?

1. Visit [vayva.ng](https://vayva.ng) and click "Get Started."
2. Enter your business name, email address, and phone number.
3. Select your industry vertical (e.g., fashion, food, electronics, beauty).
4. Choose a subscription plan (Starter, Pro, or Pro+).
5. Complete payment via Paystack.
6. You'll be taken to your merchant dashboard to begin setup.

### Q3: Is there a free trial?

Yes. Starter and Pro plans come with a **7-day free trial**. You can explore the full features of your chosen plan during this period. No charge is made until the trial ends. Pro+ does not offer a trial.

### Q4: What industries does Vayva support?

Vayva supports 20+ industry verticals, including but not limited to: Fashion & Apparel, Food & Beverage, Electronics, Beauty & Cosmetics, Health & Wellness, Travel & Tourism, Real Estate, Legal Services, Education, Agriculture, Automotive, Home & Furniture, Sports & Fitness, Art & Crafts, and Professional Services.

### Q5: What do I need to get started?

- A smartphone with WhatsApp Business installed
- A valid email address
- A bank account for receiving payments (any Nigerian bank supported by Paystack)
- Your product information (names, prices, descriptions, images)

## Pricing & Plans

|------|--------------|-------------|----------|------------|--------------|
| **Starter** | ₦25,000 | 1 | 500 | 5,000 | WhatsApp automation, storefront, basic AI |

### Q7: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. Go to **Settings > Billing > Manage Subscription** in your dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing period.

### Q8: What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all payment methods supported by Paystack: Nigerian debit cards (Verve, Mastercard, Visa), bank transfers, USSD, and mobile money. All pricing is in Nigerian Naira (₦).

### Q9: Is there a contract or long-term commitment?

No. All Vayva plans are month-to-month. You can cancel at any time, and your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

### Q10: What happens if my payment fails?

If your subscription payment fails, we'll retry the charge after 24 hours. You'll receive a notification via email and WhatsApp. If payment is not resolved within 7 days, your account will be downgraded and AI features will be paused until payment is successful.

### Q11: Do you offer refunds?

If you cancel within your 7-day trial period, you receive a full refund. After the trial, refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. Contact support@vayva.ng for refund requests.

## AI Agent & Credits

### Q12: What is the AI sales agent?

Your AI sales agent is an intelligent assistant that handles customer conversations on WhatsApp automatically. It can answer product questions, recommend items, process orders, send payment links, and provide order status updates — all without your intervention.

### Q13: What are AI credits?

AI credits are the currency used for AI-powered interactions on Vayva. Every time the AI agent processes a customer message, generates a product description, or performs an automated task, it consumes credits. Your plan includes a monthly credit allocation that renews each billing cycle.

### Q14: How many credits does each interaction use?

A typical WhatsApp conversation exchange uses approximately 1–5 credits, depending on the length and complexity. Image processing uses about 17 credits. On average, 5,000 credits support roughly 1,000 customer interactions.

### Q15: What happens when I run out of credits?

When your credits are depleted, the AI agent will pause. Your storefront and manual features continue to work normally. You'll receive a low-credit alert when you reach 200 credits remaining. You can purchase additional credits at any time.

### Q16: How do I buy more credits?

Go to **Settings > Billing > Credit Top-Up** in your dashboard. Available packages:

| Package | Credits | Price |
|---------|---------|-------|
| Small | 3,000 | ₦3,000 |
| Medium | 8,000 | ₦7,000 |
| Large | 20,000 | ₦15,000 |

### Q17: What is AI Autopilot?

AI Autopilot is an advanced mode (available on Pro and Pro+ plans) where the AI agent handles the entire sales cycle end-to-end — from greeting customers, recommending products, processing orders, sending payment links, and confirming fulfilment — without requiring merchant intervention. It uses the Llama 3.3 70B model for deeper reasoning.

### Q18: Can I customise what the AI agent says?

Yes. In your dashboard under **AI Agent > Settings**, you can:
- Set a custom greeting message
- Define your brand's tone of voice
- Add specific product knowledge and FAQs
- Set operating hours and out-of-hours messages
- Configure which products to promote

## Payments & Billing

### Q19: How do I receive payments from customers?

Customer payments are processed through Paystack and deposited into your connected bank account. When a customer makes a purchase, they receive a Paystack payment link via WhatsApp or your storefront. Once payment is confirmed, the funds appear in your Vayva balance.

### Q20: What is the withdrawal fee?

Vayva charges a **3% processing fee** on all withdrawals. For example, withdrawing ₦100,000 incurs a ₦3,000 fee, and you receive ₦97,000.

### Q21: How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawals are typically processed within 24–48 hours. The exact timing depends on your bank and Paystack's transfer queue. You will receive a notification when the transfer is completed.

### Q22: How do I connect my bank account?

Go to **Settings > Payments > Bank Account** in your dashboard. Enter your bank name, account number, and account name. The system will verify your details via Paystack before saving.

### Q23: Can my customers pay in instalments?

This feature is not currently available but is on our roadmap. For now, all purchases require full payment at checkout.

### Q24: How do I issue a refund to a customer?

Go to **Orders > [Order Details] > Issue Refund** in your dashboard. Refunds are processed back through Paystack to the customer's original payment method. Processing takes 5–10 business days.

## WhatsApp Setup

### Q25: How do I connect my WhatsApp to Vayva?

1. Go to **Settings > WhatsApp** in your dashboard.
2. Click "Connect WhatsApp."
3. A QR code will appear on screen.
4. On your phone, open WhatsApp > **Settings > Linked Devices > Link a Device**.
5. Scan the QR code.
6. Once connected, the status will show "Connected" in your dashboard.

### Q26: Do I need WhatsApp Business?

WhatsApp Business is recommended but not strictly required. WhatsApp Business gives you access to business profiles, catalogue features, and automated greetings that complement Vayva's AI agent.

### Q27: Can I use my personal WhatsApp number?

Yes, but we recommend using a dedicated business number. This keeps your personal messages separate and presents a more professional image to customers.

### Q28: What happens if my WhatsApp disconnects?

If your phone loses internet or you log into WhatsApp Web elsewhere, the connection may drop. You'll receive a notification in your dashboard. To reconnect, go to **Settings > WhatsApp > Reconnect** and scan the QR code again.

### Q29: What types of messages can the AI send on WhatsApp?

The AI agent supports text messages, images (product photos), documents (invoices, receipts), and location sharing. It can also send interactive buttons and list messages for product selection.

## Storefront

### Q30: What is my storefront URL?

Your storefront is available at `yourstorename.vayva.ng`. The subdomain is based on the store name you chose during registration.

### Q31: Can I use my own domain?

Yes, if you are on the **Pro** or **Pro+** plan. Go to **Settings > Storefront > Custom Domain** and follow the DNS configuration instructions. You will need to add a CNAME record pointing to Vayva's servers.

### Q32: Can I customise the look of my storefront?

Yes. Vayva provides storefront templates that you can customise with your brand colours, logo, and banner images. The number of templates available depends on your plan: Starter (1 template), Pro (2 templates), Pro+ (5 templates). Additional templates can be purchased at ₦5,000 each.

### Q33: Is my storefront mobile-friendly?

Yes. All Vayva storefronts are fully responsive and optimised for mobile devices, which is critical since the majority of Nigerian shoppers browse on mobile.

## Product Management

### Q34: How do I add products?

1. Go to **Products > Add Product** in your dashboard.
2. Enter the product name, description, price, and upload images.
3. Set inventory quantity, variants (size, colour, etc.) if applicable.
4. Choose a category and tags.
5. Click "Publish" to make it live on your storefront and available to the AI agent.

### Q35: How many products can I list?

- Starter: up to 100 products; Pro: up to 300; Pro+: up to 500

### Q36: Can I import products in bulk?

Yes. Go to **Products > Import** and upload a CSV file with your product data. The system supports columns for name, description, price, category, inventory, and image URLs.

### Q37: Does the AI automatically know about my products?

Yes. Once you publish a product, it becomes part of the AI agent's knowledge base. The agent can describe products, compare options, and make recommendations to customers based on your catalogue.

## Order Management

### Q38: How do I track orders?

All orders are visible in the **Orders** section of your dashboard. Each order shows status (Pending, Confirmed, Processing, Shipped, Delivered, Cancelled), customer information, payment status, and timeline. You can filter and search by date, status, or customer.

### Q39: How do I fulfil an order?

1. Go to **Orders > [Order]**.
2. Review the items and shipping details.
3. Mark the order as "Processing" when you begin preparation.
4. Mark as "Shipped" when dispatched, optionally adding a tracking number.
5. Mark as "Delivered" when the customer confirms receipt.

### Q40: What happens if a customer cancels an order?

If an order is cancelled before fulfilment, the payment can be refunded through Paystack. Go to the order details and click "Cancel & Refund." If the order has already been shipped, coordinate with the customer for a return before processing the refund.

## Account & Security

### Q41: How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address. You'll receive a password reset link via email. The link expires after 1 hour.

### Q42: How do I add team members?

Go to **Settings > Team > Invite Member**. Enter their email address and assign a role (Admin, Manager, or Viewer). Team member limits depend on your plan: Starter (1 seat), Pro (3 seats), Pro+ (5 seats).

### Q43: Is my data secure?

Yes. Vayva implements industry-standard security practices including encrypted data storage, secure API communication (HTTPS), PII sanitisation in AI interactions, and compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR). Payment data is handled exclusively by Paystack (PCI-DSS compliant) and never stored on Vayva servers.

### Q44: Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export your products, orders, and customer data in CSV format from the respective sections in your dashboard. For a full data export (GDPR/NDPR compliance), contact support@vayva.ng.

## Technical Issues

### Q45: The dashboard is loading slowly. What should I do?

1. Clear your browser cache and cookies.
2. Try a different browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended).
3. Check your internet connection.
4. If the issue persists, it may be a server-side issue — check our status page or contact support.

### Q46: I'm seeing an error message. What should I do?

Take a screenshot of the error message (including any error codes) and contact support via WhatsApp or the in-app chat. Include the following information: what you were trying to do, the exact error message, the browser you're using, and the time the error occurred.

### Q47: My product images aren't uploading. What's wrong?

Ensure your images meet the requirements: JPEG or PNG format, maximum 5MB per image, minimum resolution of 500x500 pixels. If images still fail to upload, try compressing them or using a different browser.

### Q48: The AI agent is responding slowly.

AI response times can vary based on message complexity and server load. Typical response time is 2–5 seconds. If responses consistently take longer than 10 seconds, contact support. This may indicate a rate limit or API issue that needs investigation.

### Q49: My storefront shows "404 Not Found."

This usually means the storefront build hasn't completed or there's a DNS issue. Wait 5 minutes and try again. If the issue persists, go to **Settings > Storefront** and click "Rebuild Storefront." Contact support if it still doesn't resolve.

### Q50: Can I use Vayva on my phone?

The merchant dashboard is optimised for desktop browsers. A mobile-responsive version is available for basic tasks (viewing orders, checking messages), but we recommend using a laptop or desktop for full store management. Your customers' storefront experience is fully mobile-optimised.

Multi-store capability is available on the **Pro** and **Pro+** plans. Starter plan merchants are limited to a single store.

The Visual Workflow Builder (Pro+ only) lets you create custom automation workflows using a drag-and-drop interface. You can automate tasks like sending follow-up messages after purchase, triggering restock alerts, or creating custom customer journeys — all without writing code.
