# Prohibited Items Policy

Effective Date: February 6, 2026
Last Updated: February 6, 2026

## Purpose
This policy lists products and services that may not be sold using the Vayva platform.

## Prohibited Items
You may not use Vayva to sell the following categories.

1. Illegal substances and controlled drugs.
2. Weapons, firearms, ammunition, explosives, and similar items.
3. Counterfeit goods, replicas, stolen property, or items that infringe intellectual property rights.
4. Fraudulent financial instruments, fake documents, or unauthorized financial services.
5. Adult content, adult services, or items primarily intended for sexual use.
6. Hazardous materials such as toxic, radioactive, or biohazardous items.
7. Regulated items without proper licensing, including tobacco, alcohol, pharmaceuticals, and medical devices.
8. Any item prohibited under applicable laws and regulations.

## Enforcement
We may remove listings, suspend or terminate accounts, and report activity to relevant authorities when required.

## Reporting
If you encounter prohibited items on Vayva, report them at compliance@vayva.shop.

## Disclaimer
This list is not exhaustive. Vayva may prohibit additional items to protect users and comply with legal or platform requirements.
