## Control Center Contents (All Industries)

The Control Center is a hub for website/storefront management:

```
Control Center
├── 🎨 Templates
│   └── Choose and customize storefront templates
├── 🖌️ Designer
│   └── Visual editor for storefront design
├── 📄 Pages
│   └── Manage static pages (About, Contact, etc.)
├── 📝 Blog
│   └── Create and manage blog posts
├── 🧭 Navigation
│   └── Configure menu structure
└── 🔍 SEO
    └── Search engine optimization settings
```
